# CI-GCF 2025 Socio-Economic Baseline Survey - Village Maps

Interactive maps for systematic random sampling across 25 villages in Botswana.

## Survey Information
- **Survey Period:** November 2025
- **Total Villages:** 25
- **Total Sample Size:** 2,000+ households
- **Sampling Method:** Systematic random sampling (every 5th household)

## How to Use These Maps

### For Field Supervisors:
1. Click on your assigned village link
2. Save to your device's home screen
3. Grant location permission when prompted
4. Use the location button (top-right) to see your position
5. Follow the grid numbers for survey targets
